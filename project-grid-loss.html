Project reference

Grid-loss reduction and tariff realignment roadmap

Loss-mapping, revenue protection pilots, and an affordability-tested glidepath that links tariff moves to visible service improvements for households and industry.

Tanzania Country
2023 Year

Narrative

We stitched together SCADA pulls, billing data, and feeder surveys to pinpoint where energy was disappearing. That mapping revealed a handful of high-loss clusters that were dragging down collections, so investment focused on replacing weak spans, tightening protection schemes, and proving to customers that reliability was improving.

Parallel tariff modelling introduced a glidepath that paired loss cuts with gradual revenue recovery. Each step was stress-tested against household affordability, social-tariff coverage, and lender covenants so the regulator could back reforms without overloading customers already facing high inflation.

Approach

A joint taskforce co-designed revenue protection pilots, combined SCADA data with field inspections, and published a transparent dashboard for the board and regulator. The mix of field crews and analysts built confidence in the loss estimates, prioritised feeder-by-feeder investments, and set milestones lenders could monitor alongside the utility.
