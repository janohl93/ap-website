Project reference

Grid-loss reduction and tariff realignment roadmap

Tanzania · 2023 · Distribution utility turnaround

Loss-mapping, revenue protection pilots, and an affordability-tested glidepath that links tariff moves to service improvements.

Narrative

We mapped technical and non-technical losses feeder by feeder, combining smart-meter traces with spot audits. The utility used the findings to focus on a handful of loss clusters, prioritising replacements and protection schemes that would show customers visible service gains.

Parallel tariff modelling introduced a glidepath that paired loss cuts with gradual revenue recovery. Each move was stress-tested against household affordability to maintain regulator support while keeping lenders on side.

Approach

A joint taskforce co-designed revenue protection pilots, combined SCADA data with field inspections, and published a transparent dashboard for the board and regulator. The process built confidence in loss estimates and showed where investment would matter most.
