Project reference

Operational playbooks for new cross-border flows

Dispatch, protection, and settlement playbooks that clarify accountability as new transfer capacity goes live and regional trade accelerates.

Senegal Country
2022 Year

Narrative

The interconnector linked two operators with different control philosophies. We translated treaty language into plain procedures that dispatchers could apply without legal counsel on the line, covering redispatch priorities, black-start hierarchies, and cross-border protection handoffs.

Dry-run drills surfaced misalignments between grid codes and operational practice. By resolving them before energisation, the utilities avoided the early disputes that often slow down regional power trading and built trust ahead of the first commercial transfers.

Approach

Joint operator workshops defined who calls redispatch, who pays for losses, and how outages get reported. Templates for metering disputes and data sharing were added to reduce downtime and keep regulators confident in cross-border transfers, with a simple RACI so each control room knew who owned each decision during disturbances.
