The Africa Power model

At the core of our work is a living energy-system model spanning African power markets. Assumptions are versioned, documented and tested — ensuring that each study can be reproduced and challenged.

  • Hourly dispatch modelling with security-constrained unit commitment where data allows.
  • Scenario trees for fuel prices, climate variability, policy changes and interconnector timing.
  • Transparent data lineage with sources, vintages and QA checks documented.
  • Reproducible analysis pipelines that feed concise, decision-ready outputs.
  • Dedicated views for regulators, system operators, investors and lenders.

About Africa Power Advisory

Africa Power Advisory sits at the intersection of market economics and power-system engineering. We work as an independent partner to utilities, regulators, investors and development partners.

Our team blends transaction advisors, grid specialists and data experts who build and maintain our modelling platform. This ensures that analysis, recommendations and implementation are tightly aligned.

  • Experience across public tenders, auctions and bilateral PPAs.
  • Close collaboration with engineering firms in protection, control and SCADA.
  • Lender-grade documentation, QA processes and transparent methodologies.
