Tanzania 路 Distribution utility turnaround

Grid-loss reduction and tariff realignment roadmap

Our role

Loss-mapping, revenue protection pilots, and a staged tariff glidepath tied to service improvements.

Core analytics

  • Network segment prioritisation using AMI and feeder data.
  • Tariff glidepath modelling with affordability thresholds.
  • Loss-reduction investment cases linked to cashflow.

Outcome

Clear sequencing of operational fixes and tariff moves that secured regulator buy-in and concessional financing.

  • Distribution
  • Tariff design
  • Data forensics

Namibia 路 300 MW hybrid procurement

Hybrid solar, wind, and storage competitive tender

Our role

Designed bankable PPA, bid evaluation model, and operational performance regime for a multi-technology tender.

Core analytics

  • Capacity credit and firmness modelling across nodes.
  • Revenue simulations with frequency response obligations.
  • Stress-tested developer cashflows and curtailment impacts.

Outcome

Delivered a transparent scoring and pricing approach that reduced bid spreads and secured firm capacity commitments.

  • Hybrid procurement
  • Storage
  • PPA design

Senegal 路 Interconnector readiness

Operational playbooks for new cross-border flows

Our role

Prepared playbooks for dispatch, protection, and settlement as new transfer capacity comes online.

Core analytics

  • Protection setting envelopes and ride-through criteria.

Outcome

Aligned operators, regulators, and investors on the operational rules needed for reliable interchange.
