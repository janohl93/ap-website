Designing viability gap funding for storage-hybrid auctions

Balancing tariff caps with performance-based support while protecting system needs and investor bankability.

Signals

Tariff caps alone rarely pull in storage hybrids; clarity on performance payments and de-risked availability regimes matters most.

What to watch

Credit support for performance payments, metering for storage services, and tender timelines that align funding, permitting, and grid readiness.

Toolkit

VGF sizing spreadsheets, availability and response KPIs, and a PPA term sheet that links incentives to verifiable service delivery.
